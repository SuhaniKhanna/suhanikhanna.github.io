A little about Suhani

Delhi NCR raised, Chennai shaped. Moving cities for college was my first real encounter with how beautifully diverse India is — the south/north divide challenged me, but taught me that diversity is something to be experienced, not just acknowledged.

I chose Data Science because data feels like chemistry — one form transforming into another, patterns hiding in plain sight. My fascination with psychology shapes how I read problems: not just what the numbers say, but what they mean about people.

Off-screen I'm writing, reading, crocheting, dancing, at the gym, watching a good series, or just picking up something new for the joy of it. I believe in being a jack of all trades — experience everything, optimize nothing.

"A powerful, independent woman my younger self would be proud of." — that's the work in progress.
